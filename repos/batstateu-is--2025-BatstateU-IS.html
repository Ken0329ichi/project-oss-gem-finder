batstateu-is/2025-BatstateU-IS

This repository hosts the official project files of Team J.E.E.P. from the Philippines for the FE competition in WRO 2025, detailing their autonomous, sensor-guided self-driving robot. It includes source code, system design, LEGO-to-Arduino integrations, and 3D-printed components, serving as a reference for students, mentors, and collaborators.
